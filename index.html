Suggestions for Use

For lectures, group by molecular (e.g., FH/SDH, ALK) vs. morphological themes (e.g., oncocytic, chromophobe). For reviews, expand with full-text citations and cross-references. This set is non-exhaustive (~20 references); add images or diagrams for visual aids in presentations.
